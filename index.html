Features

Everything your business needs

Zyrohs covers every part of your operations — connected, real-time, and easy to use.

Point of Sale

Fast POS with barcode scanning, multi-payment methods (cash, card, UPI, cheque), suspend & resume, and instant receipts.

Inventory Management

Track stock across multiple locations with FIFO/LIFO/Weighted Average costing, low-stock alerts, and product variations.

Sales & CRM

Manage quotations, orders, invoices, and returns. Track customer credit, agent commissions, and payment history.

Purchases & Procurement

Raise purchase orders, receive stock, manage supplier invoices and returns — all synced with inventory automatically.

Accounting & Finance

Full double-entry accounting — General Ledger, Trial Balance, P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow, and fiscal year management.

Manufacturing

Define recipes (Bill of Materials), run production batches, track raw material consumption, and calculate costs automatically.

Reports & Analytics

16+ reports: product sales, customer analytics, trending products, expense breakdown — export to Excel and PDF.

Multi-User & Roles

Assign users to specific locations with role-based access control. Manage multiple branches under one account.

Multi-Location & Multi-Currency

Run multiple stores, warehouses, or business units from a single platform with per-location inventory and reporting.
