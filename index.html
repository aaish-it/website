Features

One system. No gaps.

Every module in Zyrohs is connected — so data flows automatically, nothing falls through the cracks, and you always have the full picture.

Point of Sale

Lightning-fast checkout with barcode scanning, cash/card/UPI/cheque support, suspend & resume, and branded receipts — built for busy counters.

Inventory Management

Real-time stock across every location. FIFO, LIFO, or Weighted Average costing. Low-stock alerts before you run out — not after.

Sales & CRM

From quotation to invoice to return — track every step. Customer credit, agent commissions, and full payment history in one view.

Purchases & Procurement

Order from suppliers, receive stock, and reconcile invoices — automatically synced with your inventory and accounts the moment it happens.

Accounting & Finance

Complete double-entry accounting built in — no separate tool needed. P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow, Trial Balance, and fiscal year control.

Manufacturing

Define recipes, run production batches, track raw material consumption, and see your true production cost — automatically calculated.

Reports & Analytics

16+ actionable reports — trending products, customer performance, expense breakdown, payment tracking. Export to Excel or PDF in one click.

Multi-User & Roles

Give every team member exactly the access they need — no more, no less. Role-based control across locations, departments, and functions.

Multi-Location & Multi-Currency

Open a new branch? Add it in minutes. Run stores across cities or countries — unified inventory, reporting, and accounts under one roof.
